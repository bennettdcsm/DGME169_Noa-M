The story

Noa Tomas writes for that moment when strangers become one voice, when the bassline feels like sunlight and every drop loosens something in your chest. His melodic EDM mixes orchestral textures, analog synths, and field recordings to build immersive worlds of light. From festival cliffs to warehouse rooftops, the mission is constant: turn sound into emotion and prove that hope can be loud.
